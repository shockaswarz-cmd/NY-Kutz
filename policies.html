Studio Guidelines

Policies & Terms

Your experience at NY KUTZ is our priority. Please familiarise yourself with our studio policies to ensure a seamless appointment.

Appointments • Health & Safety • Payment • Conduct • Minors

Appointments & Cancellations

Booking Policy

  • • NY KUTZ operates strictly by appointment only. Walk-ins are not accepted.
  • • Appointments can be booked through our online booking system.
  • • Please arrive on time. If you anticipate being late, contact us immediately.

Cancellation Policy

  • • A minimum of 24 hours notice is required for cancellations or rescheduling.
  • • Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) may incur a 50% cancellation fee.
  • • No-shows will be charged the full service amount and may affect future booking privileges.

Late Arrivals

  • • Arrivals more than 15 minutes late may result in a shortened service or rescheduling.
  • • We cannot guarantee full service delivery if you arrive late, as this impacts subsequent clients.

Health & Safety

Hygiene Standards

  • • All tools and equipment are sterilised between each client using hospital-grade disinfectants.
  • • Fresh capes and towels are provided for every appointment.
  • • The studio is cleaned and sanitised regularly throughout the day.

Service Refusal for Health Conditions

To protect the health of our clients and staff, we reserve the right to refuse service if you present with:

  • ⚠ Head lice, nits, or ringworm
  • ⚠ Conjunctivitis (pink eye) or other contagious eye conditions
  • ⚠ Inflamed, swollen, or infected skin in the service area
  • ⚠ Open wounds, cuts, or sores on the scalp or face

Allergy & Patch Testing

  • • For chemical services (dye, enhancement), a patch test 48 hours prior may be required for new clients.
  • • Please disclose any allergies, medical conditions, or medications during your consultation.

Payment & Pricing

Payment Methods

  • • We accept cash and card payments (contactless, Apple Pay, Google Pay).
  • • Full payment is due at the end of each appointment.

Pricing Transparency

  • • All prices are clearly listed on our services page.
  • • Additional charges may apply for extra-long or thick hair requiring extended service time.
  • • You will be informed of the total cost before any service begins.

Complaints & Refunds

  • • Please raise any concerns before leaving the studio so we can address them immediately.
  • • We offer complimentary adjustments for reasonable complaints within 7 days.
  • • Refunds are at the discretion of NY KUTZ and assessed on a case-by-case basis.

Code of Conduct

Respectful Environment

  • • NY KUTZ is a professional studio. We maintain a respectful, calm environment.
  • • Zero tolerance for abusive, aggressive, or discriminatory behaviour.
  • • Anyone displaying inappropriate behaviour will be asked to leave.

Personal Belongings

  • • Secure storage is available for personal items during your appointment.
  • • NY KUTZ is not liable for lost or stolen belongings.

Photography & Privacy

  • • Photography or filming within the studio requires explicit consent.
  • • We may photograph our work for portfolio purposes—your consent will always be requested.

Minors & Age Restrictions

Services for Under 16s

  • • Clients under 16 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.
  • • Written parental consent may be required for certain services.
  • • Kids' haircuts are available for children aged 7 and above.

Chemical Service Restrictions

  • ⚠ Permanent hair colour and dye services are not available to clients under 16.
  • ⚠ This is for safety reasons in accordance with industry guidelines.

Right to Refuse Service

NY KUTZ reserves the right to refuse or discontinue service at any time if:

  • • The requested service is deemed unsafe, unethical, or inappropriate for the client.
  • • Hair or scalp condition makes the service inadvisable.
  • • Client behaviour poses a risk to themselves, staff, or other clients.
  • • Medical contraindications are present that preclude safe service delivery.

The final decision on service suitability rests with the barber. This is always done with your safety and wellbeing in mind.
