Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you book an appointment or contact us, we may collect:

  • • Identity Data: Your name, email address, and phone number
  • • Booking Data: Appointment dates, times, service preferences, and history
  • • Payment Data: Transaction records (processed securely via third-party providers)

Health & Safety Information

For your safety and to provide the best service, we may collect:

  • • Medical History: Allergies, skin conditions, or medications that may affect services
  • • Allergy Test Records: Results from patch tests for chemical treatments
  • • Service Notes: Recommendations and treatment records for continuity of care

This is classified as "special category data" under data protection law and is handled with additional safeguards.

Technical Data

  • • Website Usage: IP address, browser type, and pages visited (via cookies and analytics)
  • • Communication Records: Messages sent through our contact forms or social media

How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery

  • • Processing and managing your appointments and bookings
  • • Providing personalised grooming services based on your preferences and history
  • • Sending appointment confirmations and reminders
  • • Ensuring services are safe and appropriate for you based on health information

Communication

  • • Responding to your enquiries and feedback
  • • Notifying you of changes to our services or policies
  • • Sending marketing communications (only with your explicit consent)

Legal & Business

  • • Complying with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • • Protecting our legal rights in the event of a dispute
  • • Improving our website and services through analytics

Data Protection & Retention

How We Protect Your Data

  • • All data is stored securely and confidentially
  • • Payment processing handled by PCI-compliant providers — we never store card details
  • • Access to personal data is restricted to authorised staff only
  • • Our website uses SSL encryption to protect data in transit

We do not sell your data. We may share limited information with:

  • • Legal authorities when required by law

Your Rights

Under Data Protection Law, You Have The Right To:

  • 1.
    Access

    Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you

  • 2.
    Rectification

    Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data

  • 3.
    Erasure

    Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)

  • 4.
    Restriction

    Request that we limit how we use your data

  • 5.
    Portability

    Request your data in a portable format

  • 6.
    Object

    Object to processing, including direct marketing

  • 7.
    Withdraw Consent

    Withdraw consent for marketing at any time

How To Exercise Your Rights

To make a request regarding your personal data, please contact us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).
