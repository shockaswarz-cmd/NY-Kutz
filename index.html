The Man

From Ghana to London.
Driven by Mastery.

Nana, known professionally as NY KUTZ, is a master craftsman whose journey began in Ghana and was refined in the heart of London. Driven by an obsession with precision, he rejected the assembly-line approach of high-street shops to build something personal.

Today, he serves a private roster of over 250+ clients—men who understand that a haircut is not an errand, but an investment. NY KUTZ embodies discipline, silence, and the pursuit of the perfect fade.
